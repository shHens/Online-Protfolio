    Hawaii

    I have gone to Hawaii since I was about 1 years old. We used to go about every two years and would go for about two weeks. Here are a couple things that I loved doing in Hawaii.

  • I would love to go bogie boarding and standup paddleboarding on the ocean with my sister and dad.
  • On the beach I would love to build sand castels and relax with my family.
  • At night the sunsets are beautiful so I love to sit and watch the sky intill it sets.
  • We would go to a Luau and it is very special time with my family. I love Hawaii and it is my favorite vacation ever.
